# Laws of The Veil

These rules define how the supernatural world works.

## Rule 1

The Veil separates the physical world from the spirit world.

## Rule 2

Spirit Essence naturally exists everywhere.

## Rule 3

Ghosts are attracted to Spirit Essence.

## Rule 4

Every ghost has preferred essence types.

## Rule 5

Every ghost leaves behind residual essence.

## Rule 6

Every investigation leaks essence into the world.

## Rule 7

Accumulated leaked essence creates hotspots.

## Rule 8

Large hotspots evolve into major hauntings.

## Rule 9

Extreme concentrations create Hellmouths.

## Rule 10

Players never completely remove essence.

Every interaction with the supernatural has consequences.
