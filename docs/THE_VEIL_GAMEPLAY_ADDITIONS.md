# Gameplay & Progression Addendum for *The Veil*

This document complements **BOOK_OF_VEILOGY.md**.

## Core Philosophy

The player begins as a **Veil Society field researcher**, not a ghost
hunter.

Research comes first. Containment comes later.

## Scanner Knowledge

Initially:

    ANOMALY DETECTED

After sufficient analysis:

    IDENTIFIED

    Will-o'-the-Wisp

    VEILOGY UPDATED

The scanner permanently identifies known anomalies.

## Veil Essence

Ambient Veil Essence powers Veil Society technology.

Will-o'-the-Wisps are naturally occurring concentrations of Veil
Essence.

They are **not ghosts**.

## Veil Capacitor

Temporary scanner storage.

Current capacity: **5 Veil Essence**.

## Containment Cells

Permanent storage crafted from identified Veil Essence.

Current placeholder conversion:

-   5 Veil Essence
-   →
-   1 Containment Cell

## Player Choice

When the Veil Capacitor is full:

### Overload

-   Spend all capacitor energy.
-   Agitate nearby Will-o'-the-Wisps.
-   Increase local Veil instability.
-   Continue the investigation.

### Craft

-   Convert capacitor energy into a permanent Containment Cell.
-   Build long-term reserves.

## Spectral Recipes (Placeholder)

Different combinations of stored Essence determine what may manifest.

Current:

    5 Ambient Veil Essence
    ↓

    Lost Soul

Future recipes are intentionally unknown.

## Spectral Signature

Ghosts are **not** fed Essence directly.

The scanner consumes stored Essence and synthesizes a **Spectral
Signature** that is projected toward the entity.

Different entities react differently.

## Residue

Successful crossings or forced removals may leave:

-   Ectoplasm
-   Residual Veil Essence

Fresh ectoplasm can often be harvested.

## Veil Integrity

Every investigation weakens the local Veil.

Future effects:

-   More manifestations
-   Stronger entities
-   Hotspots
-   Major hauntings
-   Hellmouths

(Not yet persistent.)

## Long-Term Equipment

Working names only:

-   Resonance Projector
-   Containment Anchor
-   Advanced Veil Scanner
-   Additional Veil Society equipment

Goal:

Create a unique Veil Society style of paranormal containment instead of
copying Ghostbusters.

## Multiplayer Vision

Major entities should require multiple researchers.

Possible cooperative roles:

-   Investigation
-   Resonance Projection
-   Containment
-   Veil Stabilization
