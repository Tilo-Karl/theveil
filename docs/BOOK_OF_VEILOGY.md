# The Book of Veilogy

*A Living Field Guide of the Veil Society*

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Preface

For thousands of years humanity has observed supernatural phenomena
without understanding their true nature.

Ghosts, hauntings, apparitions, mysterious lights, and countless other
legends appear throughout every civilization. While the names differ,
the observations are often remarkably consistent.

The Veil Society exists to replace superstition with observation.

Veilogy is the scientific study of the Veil and all phenomena associated
with it.

This book records the current understanding of Veil researchers. As
knowledge grows, so too will the Book of Veilogy.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# The Veil Society

**Classification:** Organization

The Veil Society, commonly shortened to **The Veil**, is an ancient
organization devoted to observing, documenting and understanding
supernatural phenomena.

Its origins are unknown.

The Society is neither a government agency nor a secret order. It is an
informal network of independent researchers who share knowledge
accumulated over centuries.

Most members work alone.

Its purpose is not to destroy the supernatural, but to understand it.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Veilogy

**Classification:** Academic Discipline

Veilogy is the scientific study of the Veil.

Unlike folklore, Veilogy classifies supernatural phenomena according to
observable origin, behaviour and measurable characteristics rather than
myth or tradition.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# The Veil

**Classification:** Interdimensional Boundary

The Veil is the invisible boundary separating the Natural World from the
unknown Spirit World.

The Veil is **not** a place.

It is neither Heaven, Hell nor the Spirit World itself.

It is the membrane separating worlds.

Under normal circumstances every soul passes **through** the Veil only
once at death.

What exists beyond the Veil remains unknown.

When weakened, the Veil allows spectral energy, ghosts and other
anomalies to influence the Natural World.

The purpose of the Veil Society is not to cross the Veil, but to
understand and protect it.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Veil Scanner

**Classification:** Field Equipment

The Veil Scanner is the primary investigative instrument of the Veil
Society.

Modern scanners can detect, analyse and temporarily store Veil Essence,
identify known anomalies and document field observations.

The scanner does not create supernatural phenomena.

It reveals what already exists.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Veil Essence

**Classification:** Spectral Energy

Veil Essence is the fundamental energy associated with the Veil.

It naturally permeates the boundary between worlds and is normally
invisible to human senses.

Modern Veil Scanners can detect, analyse and temporarily store Veil
Essence.

Current evidence suggests that nearly every known supernatural
phenomenon depends upon Veil Essence.

Veil Essence possesses no known consciousness.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Ectoplasm

**Classification:** Spectral Matter

Ectoplasm is condensed spectral matter formed when Veil Essence
interacts with the physical world.

Unlike Veil Essence, ectoplasm possesses physical properties.

It may remain on surfaces, objects or living organisms following
significant supernatural events.

Earlier investigators believed ectoplasm itself to be the supernatural
phenomenon.

Modern Veilogy recognises ectoplasm as residue left behind by the
interaction of Veil Essence with physical matter.

Fresh ectoplasm frequently contains recoverable Veil Essence.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Will-o'-the-Wisp

**Classification:** Spectral Energy Phenomenon

Despite centuries of folklore, a Will-o'-the-Wisp is **not** a ghost.

It possesses no soul.

A Will-o'-the-Wisp is a naturally occurring concentration of free Veil
Essence displaying limited autonomous behaviour.

Historical reports of travellers following mysterious lights into
swamps, forests or over cliffs are believed to describe encounters with
Will-o'-the-Wisps.

Modern Veil Scanners allow researchers to safely capture their Veil
Essence.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Ghosts

**Classification:** Human Soul

A ghost is the surviving soul of a once-living being that has not
completed its passage through the Veil or has otherwise returned from
it.

Ghosts are distinct from energy phenomena, supernatural creatures and
other unknown entities.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Lost Soul

**Classification:** Ghost

**Threat Rating:** I

A Lost Soul is the soul of a recently deceased individual that has
failed to complete its passage through the Veil.

Unable to fully cross into the unknown Spirit World, it remains trapped
near the Natural World.

Most Lost Souls are confused rather than hostile.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Phantom

**Classification:** Ghost

**Threat Rating:** II

A Phantom retains a coherent identity and recognises itself, its
surroundings and often its former life.

Unlike Lost Souls, Phantoms frequently appear clothed and capable of
meaningful interaction.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Revenant

**Classification:** Ghost

**Threat Rating:** III

A Revenant remains because of a singular unresolved purpose.

Everything unrelated to that purpose gradually fades.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Wraith

**Classification:** Ghost

**Threat Rating:** IV

A Wraith is a ghost that has lost nearly all personal identity.

Memory, personality and reason have eroded over time, leaving instinct
and overwhelming emotion.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Poltergeist

**Classification:** Behavioural Phenomenon

A Poltergeist is **not** a separate type of ghost.

It is a designation applied to hauntings exhibiting sustained physical
interaction with the material world.

Minor Poltergeist activity may involve knocks, footsteps or moving small
objects.

Major Poltergeist events may involve violent object movement, structural
damage or direct attacks.

The term describes behaviour rather than origin.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Manifestation

**Classification:** Veil Event

A Manifestation occurs when disturbances in the Veil become sufficiently
severe to allow coherent supernatural phenomena to emerge into the
Natural World.

The mechanisms responsible remain under investigation.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Closing Remarks

Veilogy remains an incomplete science.

Every investigation expands humanity's understanding of the Veil.
