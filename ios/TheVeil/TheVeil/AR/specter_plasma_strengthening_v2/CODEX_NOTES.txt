CODEX NOTES

Purpose
- Keep the successful authored face unchanged.
- Strengthen only the surrounding purple plasma and moving electrical reinforcement.

Use
1. Keep SpecterFaceField.png from the authored-mask package.
2. Replace the current Metal shader with SpecterVFX_PlasmaStrengthening.metal.
3. Keep shader entry points named specterGeometry and specterSurface.
4. Ensure CustomMaterial.custom.texture receives SpecterFaceField.
5. Do not change combat, HUD, AR anchoring, or scanner logic.

Texture channels
- R: spectral matter/silhouette
- G: hard facial highlights
- B: cavities

Factory setup
- Add: private let faceFieldTexture: TextureResource?
- Load: faceFieldTexture = try? TextureResource.load(named: "SpecterFaceField")
- In makeLayer(), after copying the material:
  if let faceFieldTexture {
      material.custom.texture = .init(faceFieldTexture)
  }

Important
- Do not rebuild the face with circles, ellipses, capsules, straight segments, or sine-wave teeth.
- Do not add cyan fBM clouds.
- The current formation cycle is automatic only for visual testing.
- Approve the look before wiring formation to gameplay.

Main tuning points
- broadMatter multiplier: purple body density
- fineFilaments multiplier: moving current amount
- reinforcement multiplier: electricity hugging the authored face
- facialCore multiplier: white-hot detail brightness
- cavityDark multiplier: socket/nostril/maw darkness
- auraAlpha: rear plasma opacity
- faceAlpha: front face strength

Later formation values
- idle 0.15
- alert 0.40
- pre-attack 0.70
- attack 1.00
- recovery 0.20
